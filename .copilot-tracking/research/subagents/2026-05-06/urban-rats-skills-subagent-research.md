- Skills are designed for AI agents to perform complex scientific workflows using specialized libraries, databases, and tools

### Potentially Relevant Skills for Adaptation

#### Geospatial and Ecological Monitoring
- **GeoMaster**: Comprehensive geospatial science skill covering remote sensing, GIS, spatial analysis, and machine learning for earth observation
  - Supports satellite imagery processing (Sentinel, Landsat, MODIS, SAR, hyperspectral)
  - Includes spatial statistics, terrain analysis, and cloud-native workflows
  - **Application to urban rat prevention**: Could be used for monitoring urban green spaces, identifying rat habitats, tracking population spread through satellite imagery analysis, and spatial modeling of ecological factors affecting rat populations in cities like Taipei

- **GeoPandas**: Extends pandas for geospatial vector data analysis
  - Supports spatial joins, coordinate transformations, and geographic visualizations
  - **Application**: Analyzing urban spatial data for rat population management, mapping infestation hotspots, and integrating with other urban planning data

#### Population Management and Modeling
- **PyMC**: Bayesian statistical modeling and probabilistic programming
  - Supports hierarchical models, time series analysis, and uncertainty quantification
  - **Application**: Modeling rat population dynamics, predicting population growth, assessing intervention effectiveness, and Bayesian analysis of ecological monitoring data

- **aeon**: Time series machine learning with 13 algorithm categories
  - Includes forecasting, anomaly detection, and segmentation algorithms
  - **Application**: Time series analysis of rat population data, forecasting population trends, detecting unusual population changes that may indicate ecological shifts

- **scikit-learn**: Classical machine learning for predictive modeling
  - Supports classification, regression, and clustering algorithms
  - **Application**: Predictive modeling of rat infestation risk based on environmental factors, classification of high-risk urban areas

#### Ecological and Environmental Analysis
- **Hugging Science**: Curated catalog of scientific ML resources across 17 domains including ecology
  - Provides access to pre-trained models and datasets for ecological applications
  - **Application**: Leveraging ecological ML models for habitat analysis, species distribution modeling, and environmental monitoring relevant to urban pest management

#### Data Analysis and Visualization
- **Scientific Visualization**: Best practices for creating publication-quality scientific figures
  - **Application**: Visualizing rat population data, ecological monitoring results, and intervention outcomes for urban planning and public health communications

### Key Discoveries
1. **No Direct Skills**: The scientific-agent-skills repository does not contain skills specifically designed for urban rat prevention, pest control, or direct ecological monitoring of rodent populations.

2. **Adaptable Skills Available**: Several skills can be adapted for urban ecological monitoring and population management:
   - Geospatial skills (GeoMaster, GeoPandas) for spatial analysis of urban environments
   - Machine learning skills (PyMC, aeon, scikit-learn) for population modeling and prediction
   - Data visualization skills for communicating findings

3. **Ecosystem Focus**: The repository emphasizes scientific research tools rather than applied pest management, with stronger coverage in genomics, drug discovery, and geospatial analysis than in urban ecology or pest control.
