## Selected Approach

Based on research, no direct skills exist for urban rats prevention in the K-Dense Scientific Agent Skills repository. However, several adaptable skills can support urban ecological monitoring and population management:

1. **GeoMaster**: For geospatial analysis of urban habitats and population spread
2. **PyMC**: For Bayesian modeling of rat population dynamics
3. **aeon**: For time series forecasting of population trends
4. **scikit-learn**: For predictive modeling of infestation risks

These can be integrated into a Taipei city dashboard for data-driven rat prevention strategies. For direct pest management, consider developing custom skills or exploring other repositories.
